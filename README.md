# BO7 Weapon XP AFK - Cronus Zen

Automatically farms weapon XP and critical kills in BO7 Zombies 
using the aim assist perk snap mechanic combined with the melee 
perk to recover ammo and health on melee kill. The field upgrade 
is activated automatically each loop.

---

## Modes
| Mode  | Weapons |
|-------|---------|
| AUTO  | Automatic weapons, bursts, auto pistols |
| SEMI  | Semi-auto, snipers, marksman, semi pistols, Launchers |
| MELEE | Melee weapons |

**Cycle modes:** Hold LT + Press DPAD RIGHT (script must be off)  
**Toggle script:** DPAD UP

---

## Setup

**Map:** Paradox Junction - Solo Directed (Past Version)

> ⚠️ Do NOT open the barriers on the side of the yellow house 
> or zombies will no longer path correctly down the lane.

1. Start a Solo Directed match of Paradox Junction
2. Get to round cap 9
3. Get setup with all perks, 3 plate vest, upgraded weapon of 
   your choice. Light Mend ammo mod is recommended.
   Equip Frenzy Guard as your field upgrade.
4. Go to the yellow house garden on the past map, turn right 
   and go down the side of the house
5. Crouch on the fence side facing the shotgun wall buy in the distance
6. Select your mode with LT + DPAD RIGHT
7. Start the script with DPAD UP

📹 **Setup Video:** https://youtu.be/Ibo6DLUVtRI

## Notices & Known Issues

> ⚠️ **DO NOT** start the script standing up. The camera can drift 
> towards zombies on your right causing you to die. **Crouched only.**

> ⚠️ **DO NOT** start the script prone. Deadshot can snap too far 
> left causing you to die. **Crouched only.**

- If your weapon is creating crawlers, monitor the game until 
  you can upgrade to a point where it stops. Crawlers that get 
  too close can hit you but the melee perk cannot reach them.
- Paradox Junction has a force exfil at 6 hours. The script 
  auto save quits at 5 hours 30 minutes. Make sure the match 
  timer is under 30 minutes when you start the script.
- **Monkey Bomb must be equipped as your Tactical** for the auto 
  save quit to work. If you are not using Monkey Bomb, the auto 
  save quit will not function correctly.
